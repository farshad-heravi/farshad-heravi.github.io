Farshad Nozad Heravi

Robotics Engineer (Ph.D.) specializing in Motion Planning and Real-Time Control

I am a Robotics Engineer with over five years of experience in developing full-stack robotic solutions from the ground up. With a Ph.D. from the University of Genoa and IIT, my work focuses on motion planning, real-time control, and robotic perception for both industrial and space applications.

I am driven by the challenge of translating complex algorithms into practical, deployable systems. Whether it's designing collision-free paths for dual-arm manipulators or implementing sensor fusion for navigation on uneven terrain, I thrive on building robust and intelligent robots. My experience in European Horizon projects has solidified my skills in team collaboration and delivering results.
