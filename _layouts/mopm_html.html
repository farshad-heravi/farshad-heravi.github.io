Morphing Omni-Direction Parallel Mechanism

In this project, we derived the equations of the motion of a parallel, flexible, and under-actuated mechanism which was patented previously. The mechanism is a wind delivery system which captures the wind from every directions and then funnels the flow to the ground-level generator.
